## Overview

This Refund Policy is a general, editable policy for products sold by an independent developer through Yungu Studio. It should be reviewed and adjusted as final purchase channels, pricing, and licensing details are confirmed.

## General approach

If a paid product is offered directly through this site or through an authorized payment platform, refund requests may be considered on a reasonable case-by-case basis, especially when:

- The product was not delivered as described in a material way
- A duplicate purchase occurred
- A technical issue prevented reasonable use and could not be resolved within a reasonable support period

## When refunds may not be available

Refunds may be limited or declined where permitted by law, including cases involving:

- Abuse of the refund process
- Repeated purchase-and-refund behavior
- Failure to provide enough information to review the request
- Situations where a third-party marketplace controls the refund decision

## Third-party payment or distribution platforms

If a purchase is made through a browser marketplace, app platform, reseller, or payment processor, that platform's refund rules may apply first. In those cases, users should follow the refund process required by the platform.

## How to request a refund

Send refund requests to [820713556@qq.com](mailto:820713556@qq.com) and include:

- The product name
- The purchase date
- The purchase email or transaction reference, if available
- A short explanation of the issue

## Response time

Refund requests will be reviewed as reasonably possible, but no fixed response time is guaranteed on this page. This text can be updated later if a formal SLA is adopted.

## Updates

This Refund Policy may be revised as product distribution and payment flows become more established.
