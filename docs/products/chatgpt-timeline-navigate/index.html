What it does产品作用

ChatGPT Timeline Navigate helps users move through long ChatGPT conversations with less scrolling and better structure awareness. It is intended for reading long threads and jumping back to earlier sections more efficiently.ChatGPT Timeline Navigate 帮助用户在长篇 ChatGPT 对话中减少滚动、提升结构感知，并更高效地返回前文内容。

Key features核心功能

  • Timeline-style navigation for long conversations为长对话提供时间线式导航
  • Faster jumping between different sections of a thread更快跳转到对话不同片段
  • Improved reading flow in lengthy discussions改善长对话阅读流畅度
  • Cleaner browsing experience for large ChatGPT threads让超长 ChatGPT 线程浏览更清晰
